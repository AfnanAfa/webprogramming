About Us

Established in 2014 as part of the renowned KMCT Group of Institutions, KMCT Arts and Science College, Kuttippuram, stands as a leading self-financing college affiliated with the University of Calicut. Approved by the UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act 1956, the college is unequivocally committed to providing top-notch education in an environment that fosters academic and personal growth. With a wide range of undergraduate programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dedicated faculty, KMCT Arts and Science College has rapidly established a reputation for academic excellence and innovation.
